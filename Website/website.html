What is the Canadian Student Token (XCAN)?

The Canadian Student Token (known by its ticker XCAN) is a Stablecoin that is pegged Canadian dollar on a 1:1 basis. Every unit of this cryptocurrency in circulation is backed up by 1 Canadian Dollar that is held in reserve, in a mix of cash and short-term Canadian Treasury bonds.

The Stablecoin is designed for a world where cashless transactions are becoming more common. Main use cases for the token would be where finance and education come into play. It also allows students and schools to accept payments in digital assets, and shake up the sector of education.

By adding on to the financial and educational system, The Canadian Student Token ensures a safe form of payment and rewarding system.

Overall, the goal is to provide an effective digital payment while encourage students to be more disciplined in school.

Steps For Tokenizing CAD into XCAN

  1. First the user will send Canadian Dollars to the issuer's bank account.

  2. The issuer will use smart contracts to create an equivalent amount of XCAN.

  3. The new minted XCAN are then delivered to the user and the CAD will be held in reserve.

  4. To redeem the XCAN into CAD, the user can request the issuer for an specific amount by sending the equivalent amount in XCAN. (Redeeming tokens into CAD will include a 20% penalty fee if the amount is below $100. Purpose is for encouraging students to spend their tokens at school).

FAQ & how crypto currencies work

Similar to fiat money, a cryptocurrency is used for financial transactions. Cryptocurrencies are digital currencies that are not backed by governments and are mainly decentralized. It is different from fiat currencies because rather than being printed they are created or rewarded using proof-of-work or proof-of-stake.

A blockchain records and stores information such as transactions on a token or coin. This database is decentralized and what is stored can not be changed. The blockchain operates through the network through nodes or computers.

Proof-of-Work rewards users for verifying blocks done through a computer, these are called crypto miners. Proof-of-Stake rewards users for staking a coin through smart contracts by locking certain amounts up. This shows that they value a certain cryptocurrency thus creating a consensus mechanism.

DeFi or decentralized finance is financial technology that lets a user trade, buy, and sell cryptocurrencies and tokens without someone in the middle. Meaning that if there was a transaction to occur it would be peer to peer and not a bank or a broker having to charge fees to trade through them. This allows users to have more freedom financially and be substantially more secure.
